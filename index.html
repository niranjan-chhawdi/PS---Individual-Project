CMPT 461 Project

VR Assembly Challenge

Build machines inside a virtual workshop. Pick up parts with your gaze, place them onto the blueprint, and beat the clock.

Setup Options

For stereoscopic mobile VR, turn on Mobile VR / Cardboard Mode and press Enter Mobile VR on your phone.

Core Loop

Receive a build, collect parts, assemble correctly, and earn stars.

Controls

Look at a part for 1.2s to pick it up, then look at the matching slot to place it.

Challenge

Wrong placements cost time. Use hints when you get stuck, but they reduce your score.

How To Play

  1. Look at a loose part until it snaps into your view.
  2. Move your gaze to the glowing matching ghost slot.
  3. Hold your gaze again to place the part.
  4. Finish quickly for a higher score and a better star rating.
